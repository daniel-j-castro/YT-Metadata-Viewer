About

This project takes metadata from popular YouTube channels and their videos and displays the most relevant information. Each video under a channel can be viewed for it's information as well as display random comments from said video. This project is for people that like to look at numbers for YouTube videos and see if there are any sort of patterns that are displayed between things such as views and likes.
